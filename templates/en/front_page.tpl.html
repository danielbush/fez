eSpace v0.1.0 Released - 11/12/2005 - (Christiaan)
eSpace Digitial Repository software version 0.1.0 (Alpha) has been released on Sourceforge. Go and get it here.
 

eSpace is a deposit collection of papers to showcase the research output of UQ academic staff and postgraduate students across a range of subjects and disciplines, both before and after peer-reviewed publication.

The eSpace server provides free, searchable access to this research and manages its long-term archiving. UQ staff and students must register to deposit papers. Here's how.

eSpace is part of a Group of Eight initiative to make the research output of Australian Universities visible, accessible and usable through a global network of interlinked services such as OAIster. See the eSpaces FAQ for more information. Check publishers' copyright policies at SHERPA.

eSpace is part of a wider project entitled eScholarshipUQ. The aim of this DEST- funded project is to make all of UQ's research accessible via the UQ Research Finder gateway.

eSpace is running on Fedora 2.0 digital repository software, which generates eSpace archives that are compliant with the Open Archives Protocol for Metadata Harvesting OAI 2.0.

The eSpaces digital repository software is available for free at http://sourceforge.net/projects/espace/. Information and background on eSpace initiatives is available from http://www.library.uq.edu.au/escholarship
