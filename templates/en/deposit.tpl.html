Deposit

{if $isUser == false}

To deposit records in UQ eSpace, you must be logged in.

Please use the Login link if you are currently a UQ staff member or student.

External registered users should use the Non-{$smarty.const.APP_SHORT_ORG_NAME} login on the Login page. If you are a former staff member or postgraduate student, and you would like to register as a user, please contact us about obtaining a log in.

{/if}

Deposits of items should begin in the collection to which the item will belong, e.g. Publications of the Advanced Wastewater Management Centre; Publications of the School of Engineering.

To find the collections to which you have rights, first log in, then click My eSpace, then click the Collections tab. A list will appear. An edit icon will appear next to collections in which you can create records.

Click on the desired collection. This will open the Web page for that collection.

Select your desired document type from the drop-down list, e.g. Journal article, then click Create. If you do not see the document type you need, please contact us for help.

Fill in the details of the form, link to or upload a file, then click Submit for Approval. That completes the deposit process.
