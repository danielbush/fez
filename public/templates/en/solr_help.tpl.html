Advanced Search

Select SEARCH in the navigation bar above for advanced search options. (Link)

Advance Search Box Search

From the search box you may also type advanced options using Solr style search terms.

To search on a field place it in brackets
publisher:(Economic Society of Australia)

To search exact text on a field you must place it in brackets and escaped quotes
book_title:(\"Don't leave us with the bill\")

Ranges are placed in []
updated_date:[NOW/DAY-2DAY TO *]

(Although search is not case sensitive on record text the TO in a range must be in capitals. * Indicates any upper bound)

AND OR NOT can all be used (‘-‘ and ‘!’ are equivalent to NOT)

follow_up_flags:("completed") AND NOT date_year:(2011) AND institutional_status:("uq")

An even more advanced examples is
!ismemberof:UQ:237156 AND !ismemberof:UQ:247259 AND herdc_code:("C1") AND date_year:(2011) AND institutional_status:("UQ") AND updated_date:[NOW/DAY-10DAY TO *]

Quick tip -
To find all records that have a field with anything in it we use ["" TO *]
phonetic_conference_name:["" TO *]
